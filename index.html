Rules & Regulations

CLASSIFIED LEVEL 0 // FOR PUBLIC USE

Discord Server Regulations

All personnel must adhere to the following regulations while using Foundation communication channels.

No Spamming
Do not flood channels with repetitive messages, excessive emojis, or disruptive content.
No Harassment
Respect all personnel. Bullying, targeted attacks, or malicious behavior will not be tolerated.
Content Restrictions
No NSFW, NSFL, Gore, Animal Cruelty, Sexual, Sexually Suggestive, illegal, or shock content.
No DoX Attacks
Sharing personal information without consent is strictly prohibited.
Network Security
No threats of DDoS, DoS, unauthorized stress testing, or attempts to damage networks or machines.
Age Requirement
No underage users (13+ as per Discord Terms of Service).
No Trolling
Disruptive behavior intended to provoke or upset personnel is prohibited.
Information Security
No posting sensitive, classified, restricted, or other safeguarded material.
Inclusivity Policy
No racism, discrimination, transphobia, homophobia, furryphobia, xenophobia, or extremist views.
No Excessive Drama
Keep personal disputes out of public channels. Resolve issues privately or with moderators.
Advertising Policy
No advertising. The only allowed method is through the Site Affiliation Program.
No Real-World Violence
No inciting or threatening real-world violence.
Departure Protocol
If trained and wishing to leave, message The Administrator with your reason. If unavailable, contact The Vice Administrator, then Site Director, then your immediate supervisor.
Language Requirement
Use US-English only, as the server is primarily English-speaking.
Discord Compliance
Follow Discord Terms of Service and Community Guidelines at all times.

Game Regulations

All personnel must adhere to the following regulations while participating in Foundation operations.

No Exploiting
Do not use game exploits, glitches, or unintended mechanics for advantage.
No Hacking
No hacking, inserting local scripts, code, UIs/GUIs, or any unauthorized modifications.
No Trolling
Do not intentionally disrupt gameplay or harass other players.
No DoX Attacks
Sharing personal information without consent is strictly prohibited.
Network Security
No threats of DDoS, DoS, unauthorized network stress testing, or attempts to damage networks.
Unauthorized Access
No attempting to gain unauthorized access to secure systems, internal APIs, or user information.
Note: Violations of game regulations may result in immediate termination from the milsim and permanent banning from all Foundation services.

Code of Conduct

All Foundation personnel are expected to maintain the highest standards of professionalism and ethics.

Professional Behavior

  • Treat all personnel with respect regardless of rank, role, or background
  • Maintain decorum during operations and training exercises
  • Follow chain of command protocols at all times
  • Report violations of this code through appropriate channels

Roleplay Expectations

  • Stay in character during operations unless otherwise specified
  • Follow established Foundation lore and canon
  • Respect the roleplay decisions of commanding officers
  • Maintain immersion for yourself and others

Consequences for Violations

  • Minor infractions: Verbal warning and documentation
  • Moderate infractions: Temporary suspension from operations
  • Major infractions: Demotion or permanent termination from milsim
  • Extreme violations: Permanent ban from all Foundation services
Remember: The SCP Foundation milsim is intended to be an enjoyable experience for all personnel. Your cooperation in maintaining a professional environment is essential to our continued operation.
